About Us


Guitar Yuri is a premier guitar school located in London and founded in 2001. Our mission is to provide high quality guitar lessons and music education to students of all ages and skill levels. We believe that playing guitar should be fun, inspiring, and accessible to everyone. Our instructors are professional musicians with years of teaching experience. They work with each student individually to develop a customized learning plan based on their musical interests and goals.

Learning to play guitar has many physical and mental health benefits. Strumming and plucking the strings engages both hands and improves dexterity and coordination. It also strengthens finger and hand muscles and enhances motor skills.

Reading music and studying theory exercises the brain by developing connections between both hemispheres. This boosts concentration, memory, and problem-solving abilities. Playing different rhythms also stimulates neural activity.

The creative outlet of musical expression can lower stress and anxiety levels. Making music promotes relaxation, reduces tension, and elevates mood through endorphin release. Playing guitar has even been used as therapy for depression and PTSD.

As a social activity, playing guitar allows people to connect with others through a shared passion. Group lessons or jam sessions with other musicians build a sense of community and belonging. Performance opportunities also help improve confidence.
