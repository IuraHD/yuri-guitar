Course program

What will you learn

  • Lesson 1. Guitar Parts and Anatomy

    Learn the different parts of the guitar and how to hold and tune your instrument.

  • Lesson 2. Open Chords and Strumming

    Practice common open chords like G, C, D and basic strumming patterns.

  • Lesson 3. Power Chords and Tab Reading

    Learn to play power chords and read guitar tablature (tab).

  • Lesson 4. Minor Chords and Fingerpicking

    Expand your chord vocabulary with minor chords and try basic fingerpicking patterns.

  • Lesson 5. Major and Minor Scales

    Learn the foundations of major and minor scales and how to apply them to leads.

  • Lesson 6. Alternate Chord Shapes

    Expand your chord vocabulary by learning alternate chord shapes up the neck.

  • Lesson 7. Music Theory Basics

    Learn foundations of music theory like notes, intervals, triads and rhythm.

  • Lesson 8. Blues and Pentatonic Scales

    Discover the blues scale and pentatonic patterns for soloing and improvisation.

  • Lesson 9. Playing by Ear

    Tips and exercises for learning songs by ear and developing your musical intuition.

  • Lesson 10. Guitar Maintenance

    Learn how to string, tune, and do basic care for your guitar.

At Yuri's Guitar School, we excel at teaching you how to master the art of playing the guitar. Our team of skilled instructors is dedicated to helping you become a proficient guitarist, guiding you through every step of your musical journey.
